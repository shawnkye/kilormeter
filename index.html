About the Club

Founded in 2025, City Striders brings together runners of all levels for twice‑weekly group runs, occasional long runs, and monthly socials. We focus on safe routes, good vibes, and steady progress.

  • All abilities: walk‑jog to sub‑20 5Ks
  • Free to join—just show up
  • Route leaders & pace groups

Weekly Runs

Tuesday Tempo

6:45 PM • Riverside Park

5–7 km at conversational pace. Bag drop available.

Thursday Intervals

7:00 PM • City Track

Structured sets. All paces—coaches adjust reps.

Weekend Long

8:00 AM • Marina Loop

10–16 km with water stops. Option to split shorter.

*Times/locations may change for races or weather. Check our IG stories for updates.
